About Us

Who are we?

Pride Alliance is a student group at Washington University in St. Louis made for all LGBTQ+ students. Allies are also encouraged to support us and attend our events! Pride Alliance is a safe space for all students so we do not tolerate bigotry or exclusion of any marginalized groups. If you'd like to join our student group you can join our mailing list!

What do we do?

We host many social and activism events as well as have volunteer trips, invite influential speakers, and host fun charity events. We also host Suite Sundays which take place in the Pride Suite where we chill and eat sweets and enjoy each others company.
